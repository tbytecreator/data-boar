# Service tiers scope template (starter, growth, enterprise)

**Português (Brasil):** [SERVICE_TIERS_SCOPE_TEMPLATE.pt_BR.md](SERVICE_TIERS_SCOPE_TEMPLATE.pt_BR.md)

Use this template to define service tiers without overpromising legal outcomes.

## Tier A — Starter (SMB)

- Primary goal: fast visibility and first remediation backlog.
- Typical scope: limited connectors, controlled scan windows.
- Deliverables: findings report, priority list, first action plan.
- Exclusions: certification claims, legal determinations.

## Tier B — Growth (Mid-market)

- Primary goal: repeatable governance and cross-team coordination.
- Typical scope: multiple data domains, periodic re-scans, trend reporting.
- Deliverables: evidence packet, remediation cadence, governance checkpoints.
- Exclusions: legal attestation, full SOC/ISO audit replacement.

## Tier C — Enterprise Program

- Primary goal: scalable operating model and assurance-ready evidence support.
- Typical scope: segmented environments, stricter controls, phased connector expansion.
- Deliverables: structured evidence packs, control mapping support, maturity roadmap.
- Exclusions: formal certification issuance, legal sign-off substitution.

## Mandatory statement in all tiers

"Data Boar supports technical discovery, evidence generation, and remediation prioritization. Legal interpretation and compliance attestation remain under customer governance and counsel."
