# Use-case storyboard — NGO migrant welcome and humanitarian case files

**Português (Brasil):** [NGO_MIGRANT_WELCOME_AND_HUMANITARIAN_FILES.pt_BR.md](NGO_MIGRANT_WELCOME_AND_HUMANITARIAN_FILES.pt_BR.md)

Storyboard for **mission-driven** organisations holding **high-sensitivity** identity and contact data. **Ethics first:** **minimisation** and **purpose limitation** outweigh “scan everything.” This file is **not** immigration legal advice and **not** a UN or government filing.

## Cast (generic roles)

- **Organisation:** NGO providing shelter, legal orientation, or integration services; **small IT** footprint, heavy reliance on shared drives and forms.
- **Data subjects:** Migrants, refugees, family units, **minors**; volunteers and interpreters appear in logs.
- **Systems:** Intake spreadsheets, scanned IDs (where law and policy allow copies), messaging exports, case notes in mixed languages.

## Storyboard (flow)

1. **Intake spike** — staff create **urgent** folders; consent and purpose fields are **inconsistent** across volunteers.
2. **Long retention** — “just in case” archives grow; **cross-program** copies appear.
3. **Boar sniffs (narrow, time-boxed targets)** — scans must be **explicitly scoped**, **read-only**, and **approved** by accountable leadership; optional **minor** hints require human review ([MINOR_DETECTION.md](../MINOR_DETECTION.md)).
4. **Trust board** — report supports **deletion** and **segregation** decisions; product never judges immigration status.
5. **Human moment** — **Trauma-informed** handling of case notes; **counsel** for NGO-specific regulatory regimes.

## How Data Boar helps without deciding

- **Assists** **data-minimisation** workshops when leadership already committed to an inventory pass.
- **Highlights** accidental **aggregation** (too-wide roots) that increases harm if breached.
- **Does not** replace case-management systems designed for humanitarian metadata governance.

## Partner opportunity

Funders and **pro-bono** tech partners use the storyboard to scope **bounded** technical help—**not** to push maximum scanning on vulnerable populations.

## Product alignment (maintainers)

Buyer interest here is a **stress test for ethics copy** and **defaults** (narrow scope, strong warnings in USAGE). Prefer **opt-in** hints, **RBAC** on exports, and **no** narrative that implies automated eligibility or risk scoring for people.
